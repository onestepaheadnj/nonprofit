Coronary Heart Disease can affect millions per year and can cause real problems for many people. However, with better and earlier education, we can help prevent the disease and make sure that tomorrowâ€™s adults have a better chance of avoiding disease.

The children learned from a presentation about the definition of coronary heart disease and what coronary heart disease really is. They then learned about the various causes of coronary heart disease, before learning about the symptoms it poses and the complications it can cause. The presentation ended with an explanation on how to prevent such a disease.

The children were shown a demonstration of coronary heart disease. They were shown a model of what coronary heart disease can do to the heart, and how it can deeply ruin the arteries present. The children then learned about the dangers of coronary heart disease and how it affects the way the heart pumps blood.

The children then played a puzzle game where they were to answer questions correctly to get puzzle pieces, where they were given time to assemble a puzzle. They were then able to assemble the puzzle as they got more and more questions right and gained knowledge while having fun in the process.
