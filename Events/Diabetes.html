One Step Ahead

Diabetes

Synopsis

Presentation

Experiment

Game

Diabetes mellitus, the disease which affects the levels of glucose in the bloodstream, is becoming an increasing problem in modern society. However, by raising awareness about the disease, we can help prevent its spread.

Starting with a presentation, the students learned about the risks of diabetes and the simple ways to prevent it. We explained the whole deal behind diabetes and explained the fundamentals of what they needed to know about diabetes. Then, we dived into the causes and symptoms of diabetes. Then, we talked about possible complications due to diabetes before capping off the presentation with an explanation on how to prevent diabetes.

To test their knowledge, the children tested their memory with a game of “Sugar Rush.” They were first to go into a ball pit full of cards and asked to retrieve a card. In order to retain the card and to get credit for their discovery, the students were then asked a question about diabetes that was to be answered right to be able to keep their discovery.

The children were then divided up into groups. From there, they participated in a large game of Jeopardy!, where they were asked to choose various prices from categories and asked to answer questions about diabetes. The game was entertaining and engaging for the children involved, allowing them to learn and have fun doing so.
