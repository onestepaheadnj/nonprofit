High Blood Pressure

Synopsis

Presentation

Experiment

Game

High blood pressure is when the force of your blood flowing through your blood vessels is constantly too high. This is significant as this can lead to heart disease over time. This is caused by many factors, like stress, kidney disease, and old age.Symptoms can include headaches, shortness of breath, and nosebleeds. This can be prevented by regular exercise and proper dieting, among other things.

This event began with a presentation on exactly what high blood pressure was; this discussed the disease, its long-term consequences, its risk factors, its symptoms, and its prevention.

We had a small demonstration on why high blood pressure happens and what it looks like inside the blood vessels. Then the children answered questions about high blood pressure; with every correct answer, they received a puzzle piece; after collecting enough, they were able to make a puzzle. In a loose competition format, the team that finished first won, but the other teams continued.

This occurred 3 times with 3 different sets of questions focusing on different topics: prevention, causes, and symptoms. We asked if the children had any questions and asked them if they understood everything; we answered any questions they had.
