Tooth decay is devastating and with more than 30 percent of Americans brushing less than twice a day, it is important to note issues that can arise with improper dental care.

To warn the children of the dangers of diseases like gingivitis, we gave a presentation on gingivitis. We started off the presentation with an explanation of the causes of tooth decay and circumstances which can lead to tooth decay, before mentioning the symptoms of tooth decay. We closed the presentation by showing what could be done to prevent tooth decay.

The experiment showed what happened with 2 eggs, one in water and one soaked in soda for quite some time. The eggs, which were supposed to simulate teeth, showed what happened when teeth were not cared for and showed the decay possible when one does not take care of oneâ€™s teeth and dental hygiene.

The children searched for cards and learned information about different dental appliances and how they could be used to take care of their teeth. The children then learned about dental cares and foods that were present on their cards, learning how they could properly take care of their teeth and stay healthy.
